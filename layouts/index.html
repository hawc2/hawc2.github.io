About

I am a writer, editor, and translator working in digital humanities and community technology. By day I serve as a Digital Scholarship Coordinator at Temple University Libraries. I also volunteer as the Administrative Lead for Philly Community Wireless.

My work ranges from speculative fiction archives and cultural analytics to public-facing digital editions, immersive pedagogy, and collaborative arts projects. This site is a testing ground for a future personal website and a home for research, writing, and professional services.
